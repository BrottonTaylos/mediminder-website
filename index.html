Share with Carer

Keep your carer in the loop — automatically.

LookAfter makes it easy to share a health report with a trusted family member, friend, or support worker. Set it up once, and the app reminds you to send an update on your chosen schedule.

1
Enter your carer's email

Add the email address of the person you want to keep informed — a family member, friend, or support worker.

2
Choose your schedule

Pick how often you want to share — every week, every month, or before each appointment.

3
Tap Send when reminded

LookAfter sends you a notification. Tap it and your email app opens with the PDF report already attached and the address pre-filled. Just tap Send.

📧

Pre-filled Email

When the reminder arrives, your email app opens with the carer's address, a subject line, and the PDF already attached. You just tap Send — no navigating, no copying addresses.

📅

Flexible Schedule

Share weekly, monthly, or before each appointment. LookAfter sends a notification at the right time so you never forget to keep your carer updated.

📄

GP-Ready PDF Reports

Choose from a full health summary, medicines-only report, or adherence history. Every report includes a medical disclaimer so it is always used appropriately.

🔒

You Stay in Control

Nothing is sent automatically. You approve every send by tapping the notification. Your data never leaves your device without your action.

📱

No Shared Device Needed

The carer uses their own device. There is no account to create, no app to install, and no login to share. They simply receive the report by email.
