Features

Everything you need to manage your health

From daily dose reminders to GP-ready condition summaries, MediMinder covers every aspect of your medication routine.

💊

Medicine Tracker

Log all your medicines with dosage, schedule, and stock levels. Receive alerts when it's time to take a dose or reorder from the chemist.

🫀

Condition Diary

Track how you feel each day with structured diary entries. Record blood pressure, blood sugar, pain levels, mood, and more — with trend charts over time.

📅

Appointment Manager

Keep all your GP, hospital, and physiotherapy appointments in one place. Get reminders before each visit and tap to get directions.

🎙️

Voice Recordings

Record notes after appointments or when you notice a new symptom. Attach recordings directly to appointments for easy reference.

🆘

Emergency SOS

One tap to call your emergency contact. Store your GP, family members, and carers so help is always one button away.

📄

GP-Ready Reports

Generate clear PDF reports of your medicines, conditions, adherence history, and diary entries — perfect to share at your next appointment.

📊

Adherence Charts

See your weekly and monthly dose-taking history at a glance. Understand your compliance rate and identify any missed doses.

📷

Barcode & Prescription Scan

Scan a medicine barcode to auto-fill details, or photograph a prescription letter and let AI extract the key information for you.

👥

Contacts & Carers

Store your GP, pharmacist, specialists, and family contacts with categories. One tap to call or get directions to their address.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes

Getting started with MediMinder is simple — no account required, no data sent to the cloud.

1

Add your medicines

Enter your medicines manually or scan the barcode on the box. Add dosage, schedule, and current stock level.

2

Link your conditions

Select from a list of common conditions or add your own. Link each medicine to the condition it treats.

3

Log how you feel

Add daily diary entries with readings, severity ratings, and notes. Charts update automatically as you log.

4

Share with your GP

Generate a PDF report or tap the share button on any condition to send a clear summary to your doctor.
